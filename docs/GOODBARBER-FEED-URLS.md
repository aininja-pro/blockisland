## Notes

- Special characters in section names are URL-encoded (`&` → `%26`, `/` → `%2F`, `+` → `%2B`, spaces → `%20`)
- The Ferries section is not included (managed separately in GoodBarber CMS)
- In GoodBarber: section settings → scroll to Source → "Change source" → "Custom" → paste URL
- Switching to Custom does NOT delete existing CMS data; you can switch back anytime
